About Us

A Legacy of Academic Excellence

Since 2015, Saraswathi Tuition Centre has been transforming the academic journeys of hundreds of students. Our experienced faculty, structured curriculum, and personalised attention ensure that each learner reaches their full potential — whether in primary school or pursuing post-graduate studies.


We offer comprehensive coaching across CBSE and Tamil Nadu State Board syllabi, covering all subjects from Class 1 to Class 12, as well as UG and PG programmes in Arts & Science and Engineering Mathematics.
